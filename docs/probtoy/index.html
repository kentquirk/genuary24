Probability Toy

This simulates a toy that contains hundreds of little balls and a bunch of pins, plus some slots to catch them all. The balls are dropped from the top and bounce around, eventually falling into the slots. The result should be close to a normal distribution (bell curve).

In the physical toy it's quite close. It's not quite a normal distribution in this simulation, which I think is probably related to errors in the physics simulation.

Hit the space bar to restart.
