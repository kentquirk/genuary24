Layers On Layers/Black on Black

Simple motion, but we don't clear the screen each frame, just put a mostly-transparent rectangle over it to get a layer effect. After creating it, I realized that it might be interesting to also do the "black" version for Day 4's "Black on Black" prompt, so you can hit the r key (or click) to restart; there's a 1 in 5 chance of getting the black version.

Controls

  • p -- pause and unpause the sketch.
  • r or click -- restart the sketch.
