Genuary 2025, day 1 -- "Vortech"

"Vertical and horizontal lines only." An experiment in Voronoi diagrams and different ways of rendering the underlying cells. All source images are photos taken by me. Some of the renderers aren't strictly conforming to the prompt.

Controls

  • space -- pause and unpause the sketch.
  • r -- randomly change renderers and their parameters.
  • i -- select the next image.
